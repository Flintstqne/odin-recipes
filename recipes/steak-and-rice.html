Ingredients:

  • Sirloin
  • Brown Rice
  • Red Onion
  • Red Pepper
  • Asparagus
  • Olive Oil
  • Parsley
  • Kosher Salt
  • Garlic
  • Oregano

Instructions

  2. Steam the rice
  3. Add the onions and peppers
  4. Grill the asparagus
  5. Season to taste
